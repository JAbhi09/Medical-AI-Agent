│  **Possible Conditions:**                                                                                                                                                        │

│                                                                                                                                                                                  │
│  **Your Medications:**                                                                                                                                                           │
│                                                                                                                                                                                  │
│  *   **Metformin:** This medicine helps control your blood sugar.                                                                                                                │
│  *   **Atorvastatin:** This medicine helps lower your cholesterol to protect your heart.                                                                                         │

│  **What Happens Next:**                                                                                                                                                          │
